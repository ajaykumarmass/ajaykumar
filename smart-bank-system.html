Bank Rules & Regulations

  • Loan Approval: Loan approval will take up to 10 days. Proper documents must be submitted for approval.
  • Loan Interest Rates: Different loans have different interest rates. Maximum loan limits apply.
  • Deposit Interest Rates: Regular customers receive 5%, senior citizens receive 8.5%.
  • Daily Transaction Limits: ₹25,000 for withdrawals and ₹50,000 for deposits without additional verification.
  • Account Opening: Every new account must be opened with valid identification (ID proof) and supporting documents (address proof, age verification, etc.).
  • Account Activation: Accounts are activated after verifying the account holder's details. Inactive accounts for over 12 months will be subject to deactivation unless reactivated by the user.
  • Deposit Limits: The minimum deposit required to open a new savings account is $100. There is no maximum limit for deposits.
  • Withdrawal Limits: Daily withdrawals are limited to a maximum of $5,000 for savings accounts. Exceeding this amount will require branch approval.
  • Loan Application: Customers can apply for loans, but all loans are subject to credit checks and approval. Interest rates may vary based on loan type and the account holder’s creditworthiness.
  • Cheque Deposit: Cheques must be deposited within six months from the date of issue. Post-dated cheques will not be accepted.
  • Interest Rates: Savings accounts for individuals under 60 years of age offer a 7.5% interest rate. Senior citizens receive an 8.5% interest rate.
  • Banking Hours: Our online services are available 24/7, but for in-branch services, banking hours are 9 AM to 4 PM on weekdays.
  • Account Security: Users are required to set a secure PIN for account access and should not share it with anyone. For additional security, two-factor authentication is available.
  • Transaction Disputes: Any dispute regarding transactions must be reported within 30 days of the transaction date. Claims submitted after this period may not be considered.

Note: These rules are subject to change based on national banking regulations and Agri Smart Bank policies. Please refer to our official communication for any updates.
